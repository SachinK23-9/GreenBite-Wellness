Vibrant Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tahini dressing
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.
  2. Chop all vegetables and parsley.
  3. Mix quinoa, veggies, and parsley in a bowl.
  4. Drizzle tahini dressing, toss, and serve.
